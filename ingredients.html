Ingredients


  • 1 Cup of flower
  • 1 Cup of milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3/4 Cups of water
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil

How To Make

  • In a large bowl, put in flower, 2 eggs, milk, water, and salt as described above. Mix well until the batter becomes smooth.
  • On a well-heated frying pan, heat One teaspoon of oil. Then pour a ladle-full of batter into the pan.
  • Move the pan around so that the batter will spread all around in the pan. This is so that the pancakes can be thin, because this recipe requires the pancakes to be thin.
  • Once the Nale≈õnik is no longer runny, or wet-looking, on top, turn it with a spatula to the other side. Make sure each side is golden-brown in color, and maybe if there a bit darker brown. Don't let them be to light.
  • Give any topping you want, such as jelly, cottage cheese, banana, and even Nutella.

Bon Appetit!
